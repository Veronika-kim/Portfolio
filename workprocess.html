My work process:

Design thinking is at the core of creativity and innovation. It helps to build user-centered products that work in balance with technological and business requirements. By sticking to the general principles of design thinking, we ensure our focus is always on what matters the most - meeting users' needs and finding innovative solutions to solve their problems.

The design thinking process

The design thinking process is iterative and allows for flexibility and efficiency when encountering issues along the way. We start with analyzing the market, empathizing with users, conceptualizing ideas based on usersâ€™ needs and pain points, building wireframes and prototypes, and then testing with users for usability. Finally, the product is ready to be released to the public!
